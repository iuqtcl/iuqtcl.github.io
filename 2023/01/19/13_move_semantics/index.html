l-values vs. r-values

r-values

  • temporary
  • don’t have names
  • live until the end of the line
  • copy

l-values

  • not temporary
  • have names
  • live until the end of the scope
  • copy and move

only l-values can be referenced using &

r-values can be bound to const &
